FAQ

Where does my payment go? Is my card data safe?

Payments are processed by Paddle.com, a Merchant of Record handling tax and compliance in 200+ countries. PaneTrans never sees your card details — your data stays in Paddle's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

What's the difference between Lifetime and Monthly?

Lifetime is a single $14.99 payment for permanent access — including all future model updates. Monthly is $2.99/mo, cancellable any time. Lifetime breaks even after 5 months.

Can I use Pro on multiple computers?

Yes. After purchase you'll receive a license key by email. Activate it on as many of your personal browsers as you need.

How do refunds work?

2-day no-questions-asked refund window. Email hi@julineshang.win with your purchase email and we'll process it. See the full refund policy for details.

Does PaneTrans send any data to your servers?

No translation data leaves your browser. The only network requests are: (1) downloading model files from Hugging Face the first time, (2) license validation against our license server. Both are documented in the privacy policy.
